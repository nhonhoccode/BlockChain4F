About Blockchain

Our system uses blockchain technology to ensure:

  • Transparency: All administrative transactions are recorded on a distributed ledger.
  • Data Integrity: Once data is recorded, it cannot be modified or deleted.
  • Fraud Prevention: Reduces forgery and increases trust in the administrative process.
  • Easy Auditing: Allows for easy tracking and verification of transaction history.
